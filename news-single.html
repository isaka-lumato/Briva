Gold Exploration Project in Geita Shows Promising Results

Briva is pleased to announce significant progress in our gold exploration project in the Geita region. Our geological team has completed the initial phase of soil sampling and trenching, revealing high-grade gold anomalies that warrant further investigation.

The project, covering over 50 square kilometers, is strategically located in the Lake Victoria Goldfields, a region known for its world-class gold deposits. Our exploration strategy focuses on identifying structural controls on mineralization and defining drill targets for the upcoming drilling campaign.

"We are excited about the potential of this project," said the Lead Geologist at Briva. "The initial results confirm our geological model and give us confidence to proceed with the next phase of exploration. We are committed to applying the highest standards of technical excellence and environmental stewardship as we advance this project."

The initial results confirm our geological model and give us confidence to proceed with the next phase of exploration.- Lead Geologist, Briva

The next phase will involve a comprehensive drilling program to test the depth and continuity of the mineralized zones. We look forward to sharing more updates as the project progresses.
